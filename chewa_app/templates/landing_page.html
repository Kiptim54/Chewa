What is Chewa?

Chewa is a language learning web application that is designed to teach you the language of your choice, from beginner to a competent level of fluency. Our selection of languages are all local kenyan languages, starting with Kiswahili, Kikuyu, Luo, and KSL. We use a point based system so you can track your progress in each language, and have a lot of learning support.

How do I use Chewa?

1.

First create an account with us! You can use your google sign up or make a custom account with us.

2.

Go through our classes at any time, where you will go through from the base level of how to greet someone in a language as the first phase, and then how to address the family members in the second level.

3.

You will be able to hear how each of the words you have heard in your lessons are pronounced in the consecutive page to each section. Luckily we have this option available for each language!

4.

When you are done learning, you can go to home, and choose your lanuguage to quick yourself and then the level you are learning at, and collect the points you need to move to the next level!
